How it works

Credits in. Songs out.

A simple loop that makes work feel a little more fun every day.

01

Earn credits

Complete a task, hit a goal, or get recognized by your manager. Credits land in your account automatically — or admins award them manually in seconds.

02

Spend to request

Search any song from a catalog of millions and add it to the queue. Vote up songs you love, vote down songs you don't. Burn credits to jump to #1.

03

Music plays

The queue plays automatically through your office speakers. Admin controls Play, Pause, Skip. The team controls everything else.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

How do credits actually work?

Members earn credits when milestones fire — closed deals, completed tasks, manager shoutouts, anything you wire up. They spend credits to request songs, vote up or down, jump a track to #1, or guard a song from being voted off. You decide what each milestone is worth.

Do employees need their own music account?

No. Members join with a code or share link — no music account, no signup form. Only the office admin needs to connect a music source once during setup.

Which platforms can we connect for credit rules?

Anything that can fire a webhook. Salesforce, HubSpot, Monday, Jira, Zapier, Make, or your own backend. Point it at our endpoint, decide what triggers credits, and you're done. Admins can also award credits manually from the panel.

Can we filter explicit content?

Yes. The Office Friendly toggle in the admin panel filters explicit tracks from search results and the fallback playlist. One switch, applies to everything.

Is each office private?

Yes. Every office is fully isolated — separate queue, separate leaderboard, separate credit ledger. Access is gated by a join code that you can regenerate at any time.

Can I cancel or switch billing periods?

Cancel anytime from your admin panel — your office stays active through the end of the current billing period. You can switch between monthly and annual on renewal.

How long does setup take?

About 10 minutes. Connect a music source, set your credit rules, share the join code with the team, and you're live.
