How it works

Credits in. Songs out.

A simple loop that keeps your team engaged all day.

01

Hit a milestone

Close a deal, pull credit, submit a loan. Your CRM fires a webhook and credits land in your account instantly — no manual input.

02

Spend to request

Search any song on Spotify and add it to the queue. Vote up songs you love, vote down songs you don't. Burn credits to jump to #1.

03

Music plays

The queue plays automatically through your office speakers. Admin controls Play, Pause, Skip. The team controls everything else.

Features

Everything you need.

🎵

Full Spotify catalog

Search and request any song from Spotify's 100M+ track library. No limits on what can be played.

⚡

Live credit system

Credits are awarded instantly when milestones fire. Every vote costs credits. Real stakes, real fun.

🔗

CRM sync

Salesforce webhook integration out of the box. Works with any platform that can fire HTTP requests.

📺

TV leaderboard

Dedicated display mode showing who's winning the week. Put it on your office TV for max effect.

Office Friendly mode

One toggle in the admin panel filters all explicit content from search results and the fallback playlist.

Private by default

Each office is fully isolated. Join codes control access. Regenerate your code instantly at any time.
